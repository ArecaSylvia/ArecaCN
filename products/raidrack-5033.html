IDE/SATA ll to SATA ll RAID Rack

The internal RAID rack features a IDE/SATA II host attachment and SATA II device attachment. Each RAID rack model allow one HDD failures without impact on the existing data and failed drive rebuild is transparent to the host. Environmental information is accessible to either via the control panel or through serial port.

The RAID rack is most cost-effective SATA disk drive array subsystem with completely integrated high performance and data protection capabilities, which are easily integrated into servers and workstations. It can also meet the rapidly growing demand from the small to medium enterprise and SOHO market.
